Event description

The Wellness Edition is an immersive experience designed to rejuvenate mind, body, and soul. Step into a realm of holistic wellness where every element is curated to foster inner balance and vitality. From serene yoga sessions to invigorating mindfulness practices, indulge in a journey of self-discovery and renewal. Explore a variety of wellness workshops, from nutrition and fitness to meditation and stress management, led by experts in their fields. Dive into a tranquil oasis of relaxation, where soothing spa treatments and therapeutic massages await. Embrace a lifestyle of wellness and mindfulness, and emerge refreshed, revitalized, and ready to thrive. Join us for "The Wellness Edition" and embark on a transformative journey towards total well-being.

Here are three reasons why you cannot afford to miss it:

  • Self-Care Imperative: In today's fast-paced world, prioritizing self-care is crucial for maintaining overall well-being. This event provides a unique opportunity to dedicate time solely to yourself, fostering a deeper connection with your mind, body, and spirit.
  • Expert Guidance: Access to renowned experts in various wellness disciplines ensures that you receive top-tier guidance and advice tailored to your individual needs. Whether you're seeking to improve your fitness, nutrition, or mental health, you'll find invaluable insights to support your journey.
  • Comprehensive Approach: "The Wellness Edition" offers a holistic approach to wellness, addressing all aspects of your health and happiness. From physical activities like yoga and fitness classes to mental well-being practices such as meditation and mindfulness, you'll find a comprehensive array of offerings to support your overall wellness goals.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with like-minded individuals who share your commitment to living a balanced and fulfilling life. Engage in meaningful conversations, exchange ideas, and build a supportive community of wellness enthusiasts who can inspire and motivate you on your journey.

Speakers

Fridah Kisinga

Clinical Dietitian

Fridah Kisinga embodies dedication and innovation as a clinical dietitian based in Nairobi, Kenya, with seven years of enriching experience. Her journey in nutrition and dietetics, spanning both clinical and food safety realms, reflects a commitment to excellence. At Gertrude's Children Hospital, she not only ensures regulatory compliance and food quality but also spearheads patient-centered nutrition initiatives. Fridah's expertise extends beyond the hospital walls; she is a skilled content creator, leveraging her strong communication skills to educate and inspire. Fridah is poised to continue making significant strides in enhancing health and wellness through nutrition education and advocacy.
